Goals of this page
This page aims simply to share a couple pieces of math which I find especially fun/interesting, as well as a couple problems I've enjoyed! Enjoy!
Problems
Problem 1:
Given that point P is chosen on line EF such that the areas of APF and CDEP are 1 and 2, respectively, find the area indicated with a "?". Also note that ABCDEF is a regular hexagon.


Problem 2:
Given P(x) is some polynomial whose coefficients are all positive integers, and that P(1)=8 and P(16)=12345, what is P(4)?



Problem 3:
Real numbers x and y are chosen independently on the interval (0,1) (with uniform probability of selecting any one point). What is the probability that x/y rounds down to an even number?
